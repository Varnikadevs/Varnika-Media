Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our design process, timelines and collaboration.

What is your typical design process?

We start with discovery, then moodboards and concepts, followed by revisions until final delivery.

How long does a project take?

Timeline depends on scope; logos may take 3-7 days, while full brand kits can take 2-3 weeks with feedback loops.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, we include a defined number of revisions in each package to ensure satisfaction.

What file formats do you provide?

We deliver print-ready PDFs, editable AI/PSD/Indesign files, and optimized web-friendly formats.

How can we start a project with you?

Contact us with your brief, goals and any brand guidelines. We’ll propose a plan and quote quickly.
